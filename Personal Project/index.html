1.Mercedes-Benz S-Class 2019 (Stock) Complete Overview

1. Engines and Performance

  S-450

  Engine: 3.0L V6 Twin-Turbo

  Power: 362–367 hp

  Torque: 500 Nm

  0–100 km/h: ~4.9 seconds

  Top speed: 250 km/h (electronically limited)

  S560

  Engine: 4.0L V8 Biturbo

  Power: 455–469 hp

  Torque: 700 Nm

  0–100 km/h: ~4.5 seconds

  AMG S63

  Engine: 4.0L V8 AMG Biturbo

  Power: 603 hp

  0–100 km/h: ~3.5 seconds

  AMG S65

  Engine: 6.0L V12 Biturbo

  Power: 621 hp

  One of the most powerful luxury sedans Mercedes ever built.
